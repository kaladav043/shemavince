Brief background

Divine Compassion Bureau is a community based organisation formed in 2017 after an observation of the prevailing circumstances in the mother country Uganda to the different victims at different intervals. The project has its offices in Nyenje ,Mukono District . The idea has been established by very gifted mentors ,who are having good socio-economic development strategies to give a hand to the needy through ;Education , vocational skilling ,and many project hands on skills in order to produce a generation of citizens that can contribute to the development of our country by leaving no age. Founder's autobiography

My name is Pastor Musoni Geofrey. Born on 10 th ,June, 1976 in Kayiwa village Kampala District of Uganda . I grew up in a polygamous family with my step mum whom i didn't recognize first until our maid Shantel un vailed everything for me about my mum. She told me that my mum divorced with dad when i was one year old ,so the new woman was brought as care taker but later on wife. Abias Musoni was my father who fled from Rwanda during the tribal war of 1959 between the Hutu and the Tusti . When it came to the consent of my motherhood ,my mind reflected on whatever the step mum did to me i.e; i can count days i slept in the house , ate food from the kitchen. Flogging was the order of the day for any mistake and claim ,overworked at an under age

in the absence of my father .I was denied full time study at school instead sent to look after the herd .My father could not stay at home due to the hunt by Obote's Government for Rwandese suspecting them to be rebels during 1980-1986 N.R.A war. I started to think about what to do ,stranded no one to tell about my life as neither of the parents could pay attention to my voice. According to national statistics ,most children in Uganda have experienced physical violence that threatens and halts their holistic and positive development-59% of girls and 68% of boys .Gender based violence and sexual violence are also pervasive with 35% of girls and 17% of boys having experienced sexual violence during childhood. Having gone through that, i joined the army at the age of 15 years . Military life was such a hot kiln for me as i could carry heavy luggage on strict commands which healthwise affected me ,and left me with scars to remember. When alone ,i could think of my mum . I stayed traumatised and gained spiritual fear ,then spirit of rejection allover my life and from siblings. I joined bad groups which introduced me to alcoholism in order to fit in group ,faught in bars and jail became my home .Police officers started to advise me about my character for a change. When i was in the custody in 2004, a lady came in and asked if there was any one who would like to hear about God. I accepted to hear ,she promised if anyone received Jesus ,they would be released. She preached and prayed for me. What a miracle that was ! Even the fellow i had faught with had already accepted Jesus as his saviour now requesting me to follow the same trend. I loved the divine moment as we became brothers in Christ. From that previous experience ,i drill the idea of Divine Compassion Bureau to stand with any child who has no advocate for their rights ."I will speak on their behalf" . I here by seeing that single mothers being supported to look after their offspring through guidance and counselling programmes. It has been discovered that Sometimes parents leave their children for they lack food, family plan ,fees for education, and others just neglect their children which leaves them psychologically tortured forever if not taken to a rehab or guided and counselled in order to mend their mindset. I use my pain to give a green platform for the victims to lead a promising life through the activities done by the compassion. I call upon everyone who has what they can provide to save fellow african from such a mystery to stand we talk the same language and contribute towards programme accordingly .
